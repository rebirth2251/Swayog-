About Us

Health is Wealth

Swayog is a term that is made by combining two words that is Swathya(health) and yoga. In this busy world people are unable to maintain a healthy lifestyle. This leads to many problems such as poor sleep quality, indigestion, obesity, degradation of mental health and many more. These minor health issues lead to many bigger diseases such as cancer, heart attack, diabetes, etc. These diseases are not only chronic but can be fatal.Our project tries to eradicate these problems from the root by educating people about these things and also providing the necessary precaution that should be taken to avoid any mishappening that may arise.
